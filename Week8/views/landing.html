Basic Index/Guide

You can use a number of requests to access information from the server

For Week 2

  • /get + ?title=dune will give you information on some of the books we have to offer
  • /add + ?title=something will add books to our repository and give you a count on how many are there. We currently do not support deleting new books.
  • /delete + ?title=dune will remove books from our repository and give you a count on how many remain

For Week 3

  • /about will give you information on some of the books we have to offer, you may also search for a particular book here and remove a particular book here.

For Week 4

  • Linting was applied to our application

For Week 5

  • Templates are needed to serve up pages dynamically using handlebars and body-parser. We expanded on templates this week

For Week 6

  • MongoDB replaced our in-memory array for storing information. get and delete routes now use the DB to read and write to.

For Week 7

  • REST APIs to; get a single item, get all items, delete an item, add an item

    • /api/v1/books
    • /api/v1/book/:title
    • /api/v1/delete/:title
    • /api/v1/add/:title
